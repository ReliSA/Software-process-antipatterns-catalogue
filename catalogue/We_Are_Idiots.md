# We Are Idiots

## Also Known As

Blame culture organizations

## Summary

There isn’t autonomy inside an organization, everything needs superior approval.

## Context

There isn’t autonomy inside an organization, everything needs superior approval.

## Symptoms and Consequences

-	Demotivation from individuals.
-	Tasks take a lot of time to be done.
-	Less innovation.
-	Reduces job satisfaction
-	Increases stress and anxiety.

## Causes

-	Organizations don’t trust the employees. 
-	Emotional reactions by the manager.
-	Public shaming.
-	Leaders don’t want to take responsibility.

## Solution

-	A positive view of a mistake. It’s an opportunity to get better.
-	Leaders take more responsibility.
-	Culture of trust inside an organization.
