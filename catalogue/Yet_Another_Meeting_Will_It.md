# Yet Another Meeting Will Solve It

## Also Known As

n/a

## Summary

View of a meeting to solve problems inside a project.

## Context

If something is wrong, the view to solution is another meeting instead of real resolutions. This meeting probably will delay the project and decrease productivity.

## Symptoms and Consequences

-	Time that can be used to real work is used to discussion.
-	Productivity decreases.
-	Reduced autonomy.
-	Extra hours to compensate for the ones wasted on the meeting.
-	Increases stress.

## Causes

-	Big teams turn collaboration harder.
-	Lack of communication.
-	Fear of exclusion in important meetings.

## Solution

-	Every meeting should be only an essential part of the team.
-	Act over another discussion.
-	Try to solve the problem via a simple way (email, messageâ€¦).
-	Establish a total of meeting hours per week.
-	Establishment of a solid routine.
