# Work Estimates Given to Teams

## Also Known As

n/a

## Summary

It happens when someone schedules the amount of hours of task and he/she isn’t doing the task.

## Context

It is established a deadline to develop, sell… And the one who established isn’t doing the job that was established by himself/herself. This can lead to a lot of misunderstandings.

## Symptoms and Consequences

-	Wrong deadlines.
-	Guilt of failure sometimes is given to the worker.
-	High pression to do tasks.
-	Extra costs.

## Causes

-	No discussion between members of the team.
-	The manager isn’t open to discussion.

## Solution

-	Discussion with team about the complexity and scheduling information to develop what is present.
-	Use of historical data to prepare similar tasks
-	Small subtasks scheduling will help in better scheduling to the whole project.
