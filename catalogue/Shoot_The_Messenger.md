# Shoot The Messenger

## Also Known As

Visibility Gets You Shot

## Summary

Toxic leadership where managers blame the employees.

## Context

The employees receive the blame for the unsuccessful project. They are penalized for everything bad that happens during the project.

## Symptoms and Consequences

-	Fear and silence from employees.
-	Employees may hide problems / Lack of transparency.
-	Self-protection by the managers.
-	Lack of Awareness.
-	Poor decision making.

## Causes

-	Toxic leadership.

## Solution

-	The manager should not react aggressively.
-	Create a safer environment to speak and share what is happening

## Notes

Once this and the antipattern “Visibility Gets You Shot” share some information in common they complete themselves.
