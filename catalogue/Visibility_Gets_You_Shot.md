# Visibility Gets You Shot

## Also Known As

n/a

## Summary

The blame falls on employees. 

## Context

Employees try to hide the problems because sharing them will get them in trouble. They are blamed and criticized for failing to achieve the target.

## Symptoms and Consequences

-	Project seem better when they are saw from the outside because the employees are not sharing the real status of it.
-	Blame culture is created.
-	Last hour surprises.
-	People stop trying to innovate.
-	Data about the project is manipulated.

## Causes

-	High pression by the manager.
-	Toxic environment inside of the organization

## Solution

-	Creation of a good environment where people will not be afraid of the error.
-	Change of mindset, mistakes are a part of the process.
-	Managers should take more responsibility.

## Notes

Once this and the antipattern “Shoot the Messenger” share some information in common they complete themselves.
