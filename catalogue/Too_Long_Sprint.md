# Too Long Sprint

## Also Known As

n/a

## Summary

The end date of the project is scheduled ahead of what it needs.

## Context

This happen because the team is afraid of missing deadlines in tasks, leading to complexity, waste of timeâ€¦

## Symptoms and Consequences

-	Complexity.
-	Scope creep.
-	Loss of stakeholder trust.
-	More work can lead to more errors.
-	Slows feedback.
-	Laziness of the team.
-	Procrastination and lack of concentration.

## Causes

-	Poor planning.
-	Overcommitment.

## Solution

-	Shorter the deadline.
-	Focus on completed work, not on work the team has already started.
-	Constant deployment.
